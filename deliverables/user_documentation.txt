This documentation brief describes how to use our website

# Login & Registration
In the [welcome page](http://localhost:5000/index), click "Login" Button to login, or click the "Register" to register a new user.

"TestUpload" are used only for test purpose. 
**Note: The User has to be a registered user in TestUpload.**

# Upload an Image
After login, you may upload a image by click "Upload" button. Supported image format are **png, jpg, jpeg, gif**, other file will not be accepted.

# Browse Images
After login, you may browse you Images by click "Browse" button. A list of thumbnails of your images are displayed. Click on any one of them will display the original image, as well as the three transformed images.
